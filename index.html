Founded in 2013 and backed by more than 20 years of industry experience, dERACLE provides industrial memory and display solutions for customers worldwide.

As an Official Supply Partner of KOWIN products, we supply DRAM modules, SSDs, and embedded memory solutions for industrial, gaming, automation, and embedded applications.

Our strength lies in delivering flexible support, competitive sourcing, and responsive service, from small-volume projects to large-scale production programs.
