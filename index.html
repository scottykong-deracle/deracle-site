Company

Deracle, founded in 2013 with over 20 years of industry experience, specializes in custom touch screen assembly and optimized display solutions.

While we are fully capable of supporting mass production, our core strength lies in delivering flexible and customized solutions tailored to each customer’s specific requirements.

We offer flexible production capabilities, from small-volume customized projects to larger-scale supply, with fast and responsive support to meet diverse customer needs.

Solutions

Custom Display Solutions

End-to-end custom display solutions from panel selection to final integration. We support optimized design, mechanical structure, and controller development to ensure reliable performance across various applications.

Touch Integration

Integrated various touch solutions including PCAP and resistive technologies, with both air bonding and optical bonding options. Designed for durability, accuracy, and seamless user interaction in demanding environments.

Industrial & Gaming Displays

High-reliability display solutions tailored for industrial and gaming applications. Optimized for performance, durability, and long-term operation in harsh environments.

Engineering Support

Comprehensive engineering support including firmware optimization, signal tuning, panel sorting, and color calibration to enhance overall display performance and ensure stable system integration.
