Introduction

DERACLE supplies embedded memory solutions through its partnership with KOWIN, offering reliable DRAM IC and embedded flash products for industrial and commercial applications.


Our embedded memory portfolio includes DDR3L SDRAM, DDR4 SDRAM, LPDDR4, LPDDR4X and eMMC 5.1 solutions designed for AI Edge Computing, Industrial PCs, Medical Equipment, POS Systems, IoT Devices and Embedded Systems with long-term product availability.
