Case Study: eMMC PCB Fanout Optimization and Validation

[Phase 1: Technical Inquiry]
A client requested confirmation on whether 'Not Connected' (NC) pins on an eMMC BGA footprint could be shorted to adjacent lines to simplify their PCB routing.

[Phase 2: Technical Analysis]
We verified that the specified NC pins are internally disconnected, confirming they can be safely used for routing without affecting device operation.

[Phase 3: Strategic Action]
During the layout review, we proactively identified a missing Data Strobe (DS) connection for HS400 performance and supplied verified samples to ensure seamless prototype testing.

[Phase 4: Result & Conclusion]
The client successfully integrated the samples into their manufacturing process, ensuring both design flexibility and optimized performance for their final application.
