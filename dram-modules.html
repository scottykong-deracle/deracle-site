Frequently Asked Questions

+ What are the key differences between DDR4 and DDR5?

DDR5 offers higher bandwidth, improved power efficiency, and integrated ECC, making it ideal for next-gen high-performance systems.

+ What is the benefit of ECC in industrial DRAM?

ECC (Error Correction Code) automatically detects and fixes data corruption, ensuring system stability and reliability in mission-critical industrial applications.

+ What is the difference between UDIMM, SODIMM, and RDIMM?

These refer to form factors: SODIMM is for compact systems like NUCs, UDIMM is for standard desktops, and RDIMM is typically designed for server-grade applications.

+ Why is operating temperature critical for industrial memory?

Industrial-grade DRAM is built to withstand extreme thermal conditions (-40°C to +85°C), ensuring continuous operation where standard modules would fail.
